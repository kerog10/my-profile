About Me

My curiosity for technology started the day my dad brought home a Pentium 1 desktop, which ran MS-DOS, and one of the most epic games of the 90s, Duke Nukem. My love for technology has grown from that point on, to become my career passion.

Being a software developer is to be involved in a world that is continuously changing at a rapid speed. This has driven me to want to grow in my knowledge and capabilities within the industry. I strive to create bespoke software for the purpose of adding value to the world around me. My driving force is to use my skill set to make people's lives easier in whatever way I can.

Humility, integrity and the willingness to help others are some of the values I try to add to everything I do.
